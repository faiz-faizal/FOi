About Us


What is FOi?

FOi is stand for Food Ordering IPB.

Who invent FOi?

FOi was invented by two students of Diploma Science Computer.

When was FOi invented?

FOi was invented in their last semester as the final year project.

Why FOi?

The problem of ordering manually written at a piece of paper of what the menu is and the name of the customer. Then, the paper will be pass to the chef to cook and packaging. Next, the waiter will call the customer’s name and charge based on the order. The disadvantages of manually written is;

1. There will be misunderstanding of calling the customer’s name.
2. Paper is light-weight so it will sure to be either blown away or disappeared before anyone eyes.
3. The waiter will be charge the customer based on their mood, seriously, mood.

That is why, FOi is invented. To help the management of food stalls more simple, systematic and convenient. Plus, FOi is mainly helping customers that mostly are students and lecturers IPB to manage their meal schedule. FOi is giving the customers to review the menus from food stalls as they can set time and pick up their order without getting rush-hour with crowds. FOi even provide the receipt for the customers to check the prices and orders are same as there would be no falsify. FOi even giving customers the choice to add or remove order before successfully submit. Therefore, they may choose the food wisely and nicely.
